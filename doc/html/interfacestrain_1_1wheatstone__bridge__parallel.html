Computes the output of a wheatstone bridge when two gages are in parallel in each leg of the bridge.

Computes the output of a wheatstone bridge where two strain gages are placed in parallel in each leg.

Parameters
[in]fgThe gage factor of each strain gage. Each gage is assigned the same gage factor.
[in]strain1aThe strain in leg 1a of the bridge.
[in]strain1bThe strain in leg 1b of the bridge.
[in]strain2aThe strain in leg 2a of the bridge.
[in]strain2bThe strain in leg 2b of the bridge.
[in]strain3aThe strain in leg 3a of the bridge.
[in]strain3bThe strain in leg 3b of the bridge.
[in]strain4aThe strain in leg 4a of the bridge.
[in]strain4bThe strain in leg 4b of the bridge.
Returns
The output of the bridge, in units of mV/V.
Remarks
The construction of the wheatstone bridge is as follows. 
             V(+)
    Leg 1b   /\  Leg 4b
            /  \
  Leg 1a   /    \  Leg 4a
 Vout(-) _/      \_ Vout(+) 
  Leg 2b  \      / Leg 3b
           \    /
    Leg 2a  \  /   Leg 3a
             \/
            V(-)
The bridge output is computed similarily to that of a traditional wheatstone bridge, but the resistance of each leg requires treatment of two strain gages in parallel. The gage resistance in each leg is as follows.
\( R_{eff,i} = \frac{R_{a,i} R_{b,i}}{R_{a,i} + R_{b,i}} \)
Where
\( R_{a,i} = R (f_g \epsilon_{a,i} + 1) \), and \( R_{b,i} = R (f_g \epsilon_{b,i} + 1) \).
