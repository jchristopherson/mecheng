Computes the output of a wheatstone bridge.

Parameters
[in]fgThe gage factor of each strain gage. Each gage is assigned the same gage factor.
[in]strain1The strain in leg 1 of the bridge.
[in]strain2The strain in leg 2 of the bridge.
[in]strain3The strain in leg 3 of the bridge.
[in]strain4The strain in leg 4 of the bridge.
Returns
The output of the bridge, in units of mV/V.
Remarks
The construction of the wheatstone bridge is as follows. 
            V(+)
            /\
    Leg 1  /  \ Leg 4
 Vout(-) _/    \_ Vout(+)
          \    /
    Leg 2  \  / Leg 3
            \/
            V(-)
The output of the bridge assuming each gage has the same resistance and the same gage factor is as follows.
\( \frac{V_{out}}{V} = \frac{f_{g}(f_{g}(\epsilon_{1}\epsilon_{3} - \epsilon_{2}\epsilon_{4}) - (\epsilon_{4} - \epsilon_{3} + \epsilon_{2} - \epsilon_{1}))}{4 + f_{g}(f_{g}(\epsilon_{3} (\epsilon_{2} + \epsilon_{1}) + \epsilon_{4} (\epsilon_{2} + \epsilon_{1})) + 2 (\epsilon_{1} + \epsilon_{2} + \epsilon_{3} + \epsilon_{4}))} \)

Computes the output of a wheatstone bridge.

Parameters
[in]fgA 4-element array containing the gage factor for each strain gage (gage 1, gage 2, gage 3, gage 4).
[in]rA 4-element array containing the resistance for each strain gage (gage 1, gage 2, gage 3, gage 4).
[in]strain1The strain in leg 1 of the bridge.
[in]strain2The strain in leg 2 of the bridge.
[in]strain3The strain in leg 3 of the bridge.
[in]strain4The strain in leg 4 of the bridge.
Returns
The output of the bridge, in units of mV/V.
Remarks
The construction of the wheatstone bridge is as follows. 
            V(+)
            /\
    Leg 1  /  \ Leg 4
 Vout(-) _/    \_ Vout(+)
          \    /
    Leg 2  \  / Leg 3
            \/
            V(-)
The output of the bridge is defined as follows.
\( \frac{V_{out}}{V} = \frac{R_{3} + \Delta R_{3}}{R_{3} + \Delta R_{3} + R_{4} + \Delta R_{4}} - \frac{R_{2} + \Delta R_{2}}{R_{2} + \Delta R_{2} + R_{1} + \Delta R_{1}}\)
Where: \( \Delta R_{i} = f_{g,i} R_{i} \epsilon_{i}, i = 1..4 \).

Computes the output of a wheatstone bridge where two strain gages are placed in parallel in each leg.

Parameters
[in]fgThe gage factor of each strain gage. Each gage is assigned the same gage factor.
[in]strain1aThe strain in leg 1a of the bridge.
[in]strain1bThe strain in leg 1b of the bridge.
[in]strain2aThe strain in leg 2a of the bridge.
[in]strain2bThe strain in leg 2b of the bridge.
[in]strain3aThe strain in leg 3a of the bridge.
[in]strain3bThe strain in leg 3b of the bridge.
[in]strain4aThe strain in leg 4a of the bridge.
[in]strain4bThe strain in leg 4b of the bridge.
Returns
The output of the bridge, in units of mV/V.
Remarks
The construction of the wheatstone bridge is as follows. 
             V(+)
    Leg 1b   /\  Leg 4b
            /  \
  Leg 1a   /    \  Leg 4a
 Vout(-) _/      \_ Vout(+) 
  Leg 2b  \      / Leg 3b
           \    /
    Leg 2a  \  /   Leg 3a
             \/
            V(-)
The bridge output is computed similarily to that of a traditional wheatstone bridge, but the resistance of each leg requires treatment of two strain gages in parallel. The gage resistance in each leg is as follows.
\( R_{eff,i} = \frac{R_{a,i} R_{b,i}}{R_{a,i} + R_{b,i}} \)
Where
\( R_{a,i} = R f_g (\epsilon_{a,i} + 1) \), and \( R_{b,i} = R f_g (\epsilon_{b,i} + 1) \).

Parameters

The output of the bridge, in units of mV/V.
Remarks
The construction of the wheatstone bridge is as follows. 
             V(+)
    Leg 1b   /\  Leg 4b
            /  \
  Leg 1a   /    \  Leg 4a
 Vout(-) _/      \_ Vout(+) 
  Leg 2b  \      / Leg 3b
           \    /
    Leg 2a  \  /   Leg 3a
             \/
            V(-)
The bridge output is computed similarily to that of a traditional wheatstone bridge, but the resistance of each leg requires treatment of two strain gages in series. The gage resistance in each leg is as follows. \( R_{eff,i} = f_{a,i} R_{a,i} \epsilon_{a,i} + R_{a,i} + f_{b,i} R_{b,i} \epsilon_{b,i} + R_{b,i} \)
